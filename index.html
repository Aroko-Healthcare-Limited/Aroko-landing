One Account, Different Use

Individual

Manage your personal healthcare plan effortlessly. Select from our range of plans—Aroko Advantage, Aroko Essential, or Aroko Premium—to suit your health requirements. With your account, you can stay updated on your health journey.

Someone Else

Extend the gift of health to others. Our platform allows you to subscribe to a healthcare plan on behalf of someone else, such as a neighbor or a community member in need. Simply provide their necessary details, choose the appropriate plan, and manage their healthcare services through your account.

Friends & Family

Ensure your loved ones receive the best care by managing their subscriptions under your account. Whether it's for family members or close friends, you can oversee their healthcare plans, monitor appointments, and keep track of their medical needs, all in one place.
